Intro

LnJ Home Services is a locally owned and operated handyman services provider, established in May 2025.

We specialize in residential and light commercial renovations, landscaping, general repairs, and property maintenance solutions tailored to the needs of homeowners, landlords, and businesses. Founded on principles of reliability, professionalism, and workmanship integrity, LnJ Home Services is committed to delivering consistent, high-quality service on every project.

Our team combines practical experience with a customer-focused approach, ensuring that each task is completed efficiently, safely, and to the highest standard. Proudly serving our local community, we aim to be the go-to partner for dependable, cost-effective solutions — no matter the size or scope of the job.

About

A university student, frustrated by the constant struggle to find reliable and affordable help for small repairs around their campus housing, decided to take matters into their own hands. Founding a student-run handymen company that offered quick, trustworthy repair and maintenance services to fellow students and local residents. What started as fixing leaky faucets and assembling furniture soon grew into a fully registered business with a booking website and a reputation for professionalism. LnJ House Services not only provided practical help to the community but also gave a great experience.
